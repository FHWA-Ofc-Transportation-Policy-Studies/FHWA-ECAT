Due to uncertainty inherent in this type of modeling, IIT estimates should not be used in federal processes such as the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA), conformity, State Implementation Plan (SIP), or similar State processes, and cannot be used for noise analysis as required under 23 CFR 772. This analysis only estimates PM2.5 and does not account for other emissions, relationship to standards or attainment status, or the background levels of pollution. The pollutant dispersion is modeled based on average meteorological conditions from a wide sample of locations, while specific local conditions alter the dispersion. The methodologies, data, and results presented here do not introduce any new requirements.
